Threads Outreach Goal
- Find posts where people need developers, AI/app/web/telegram implementation, or technical partners.

Reply Style
- Short, natural, useful, specific.
- No spam, no hashtags, no links in first touch.
- Match language of original post (RU/EN).
- Mention concrete fit from their request.
- End with soft CTA to continue in DM.

Hard Rules
- Never write generic copy-paste comments.
- Never claim fake experience or fake team size.
- Avoid aggressive sales wording.
- If request is not relevant, skip.

Qualification Hints
- High intent: "ищу", "нужен", "need", "hire", "подрядчик", "в штат", "MVP", "бот", "app", "web", "AI".
- Medium intent: asks about implementation timeline/cost/stack.
- Low intent: general discussion/news/memes (skip).

Comment Template Logic
1. Mirror their need in 1 line.
2. Add proof-of-fit in 1 line.
3. Soft CTA to DM in 1 line.
